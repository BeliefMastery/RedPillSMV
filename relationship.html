Extended Explanation

What you'll discover

  • Points of Conflict: 20 compatibility points (impact tiers: Very High, High, Moderate-High, Moderate); strain points ranked by priority and severity; “What this is” and long-term impact per area
  • Archetypal pressures (Coalition Rank, Reproductive Confidence, Axis of Attraction) where relevant to each strain point
  • Action strategies per strain point: self-regulation, relational invitations, structural boundaries, change vs. acceptance—only where specific to that area
  • Relationship Viability Evaluation: Six dimensions (future potential, temporary vs. pattern, energy investment, shared convictions, indicators of a worthwhile relationship, decision clarity); overall conclusion (worth investing / unclear / consider stepping away); summary of your responses by dimension
  • Closure and next steps; export in JSON or CSV for AI integration

Theory: Strain points and viability

This engine identifies pressure points in relational structure. It does not determine fault or whether a relationship should continue—it reflects your experience. The conflict assessment surfaces strain and targeted strategies; the viability evaluation reflects the six considerations that inform whether investing in resolution is likely to bring long-term value. Draws on relationship dynamics, polarity, and mating-market framing.

🔗 Explore further: Strain points often root in identity, polarity, or market reality. Polarity Position Mapping clarifies fit; Modern Archetype Identification surfaces archetypal pressures; Attraction & Status maps mating-market position.
