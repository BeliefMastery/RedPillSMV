Theory: Strain points and viability

This engine identifies pressure points in relational structure. It does not determine fault or whether a relationship should continue—it reflects your experience. The conflict assessment surfaces strain and targeted strategies; the viability evaluation reflects the six considerations that inform whether investing in resolution is likely to bring long-term value. Draws on relationship dynamics, polarity, and mating-market framing.
