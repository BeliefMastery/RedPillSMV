Theory: Red-pill attraction and status

Three undercurrents: Coalition Rank (intrasex standing); Reproductive Confidence (childrearing-demand calculation—women filter for provision/protection, men for loyalty/nurture/trust); Axis of Attraction (immediate display filter). Male clusters: 3C's (Courage, Control, Competence), 4P's (Perspicacity, Protector, Provider, Parental Investor), Axis. Female clusters: 3S's (Social Influence, Selectivity, Status Signaling), Reproductive Confidence, Axis. Hypergamy seeks upward convergence; Commitment Securing seeks stability.

Rad Activity (men, weighted): Scored within Axis of Attraction. Activity type (40%): anti-rad (porn, drugs, gaming, TV) = consumption/escape, attraction-killing; low-rad (badminton, casual hobbies) = weak signal; rad (snowboarding, kitesurfing, martial arts) = risk, skill, lifestyle; high-rad (business, mission) = "fucking the world with your passion." Consumption vs creation (30%), competition (20%), visibility (10%). Anti-rad activities cap the score at 25th percentile. When significant, the impact is exposed in the report.

This exposes leverage, not identity—states are time-sensitive.
